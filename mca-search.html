About CharteredOne Score

Tofler Score measures the quality of performance and integrity of a company. It is dependent on three key factors - Sustainability, Governance, and Growth. To know more about these factors,

Sustainability is a measure of overall financial health of the company. It indicates its profitability, efficiency of operations and effective use of funds and assets deployed in the business. It also reflects the company's ability to cover its liabilities and pay off its suppliers and creditors on time. Governance is a measure of the quality of corporate governance of the company. It covers aspects like compliance with TDS, GST and other laws, potential for conflict of business interests, defaulter lists checks, financials trust checks, and other checks to measure if the company is operating in best faith. Growth measures the growth capability of the company. It measures how fast the company has been growing and whether it can keep growing sustainably.
